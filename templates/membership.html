HOW TO BE A BUYER

To be a buyer is an easy husle free endevour that will help make your growth and continued relationship with us better. To be a buyer, you must:

  • Register with us. register here
  • Choose a company that sells what you need in the privilege dropdown as on the registration page. Choose from:
    • BT TITAN
    • FLINTWOOD
    • BIOTEC
    • ALL(All three companies)
  • Now pick buyer from the role dropdown list.
  • Provide us with your email and phone number and all other relevant information.

HOW TO BEGIN SHOPPING

Now that you have registered you will be directed to the homepage where you will see some products provided by the different companies depending on your privilege choice you may view either of the three or all if you choose the privilege as all.
